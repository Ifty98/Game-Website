Let's learn how to play

> Space paddle is played as a normal ping pong game but with some different rules.

> You play against the computer.

> The computer will not make any mistakes. Your score depends on how long you can last playing against the computer.

> You will only have 5 attempts so take advantage of them because if you fail 5 times the game is over and your score will be reset.

> But it sounds too simple right? so to increase the difficulty every 10 seconds the speed of the ball will increase.
